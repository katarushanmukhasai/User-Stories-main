User Stories:

As a user, I want to be able to open a DMAT account and link it to my SB account, so that I can buy and sell shares online.
As a user, I want to be able to view my shares portfolio in my DMAT account, so that I can track my investments.
As a user, I want to be able to search for stocks in the stock market and view their current market price, so that I can decide which stocks to buy.
As a user, I want to be able to place a buy order for a specific quantity of shares at market price, so that I can purchase shares instantly.
As a user, I want to be able to place a buy order for a specific quantity of shares at a limit price, so that I can purchase shares at a desired price point.
As a user, I want to be able to place a sell order for a specific quantity of shares at market price, so that I can sell shares instantly.
As a user, I want to be able to place a sell order for a specific quantity of shares at a limit price, so that I can sell shares at a desired price point.
As a user, I want to be able to view my transaction history, so that I can keep track of my buy and sell orders.



Issues:

Integration of SB and DMAT accounts for seamless transactions.
Real-time stock market data to show current market prices.
Handling of blocked amount for buy orders.
Confirmation of buy and sell orders.
Transfer of shares and funds between accounts.
Calculation and deduction of commission for transactions.
Secure authentication and authorization for user accounts.
Handling of errors and exceptions during transactions.
